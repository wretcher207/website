ReaScript · REAPER
DeHumanizer Pro

A REAPER ReaScript for intentional dynamics shaping in modern drum programming. Features two independent engines: a Velocity Engine with time-mapped drawable curves, role-aware filtering (Kick, Snare, Cymbals, etc.), smart phrase reset logic, velocity preview, and full settings persistence; and a Timing Engine with per-kit-piece lean (rush/drag) and variance, BPM-aware timing tooltips, drawable per-role scatter curves, and beat-aware tightening on beats 1–4. Distributed via ReaPack.

ReaScript · REAPER
Dead Pixel Drum Apparatus

A REAPER ReaScript that generates heavy-music drum MIDI patterns directly inside REAPER. Includes 43 grooves across 12 genre categories: thall, djent, death metal, slam death, black metal, grindcore, metalcore, doom/sludge, progressive metal, thrash, and breakdowns. Features 4 MIDI map presets with an in-script editor, power hand control, configurable time signatures, humanize/push-pull timing, and auto tom fills. Requires REAPER 6.0+ and ReaImGui 0.8+.
